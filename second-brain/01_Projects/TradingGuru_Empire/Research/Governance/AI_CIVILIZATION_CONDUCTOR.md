## Section III — Civilization Mode

4 ecosystem-wide modes:

### Expansion Civilization
Conditions: Macro Stability > 80 · Meta Health > 85 · DD < 2% · Liquidity expansion.
Behavior: multi-agent aggressive coordination · capital rotation acceleration · research funding ↑ · progressive scaling.

### Optimization Civilization
Conditions: Macro stable/neutral · Meta Health 75-85 · No major risk signals.
Behavior: balanced aggression · strategy refinement · research prioritization · capital discipline.

### Defensive Civilization
Conditions: Macro instability rising · Regime transition prob > 60% · DD 3-5%.
Behavior: forced SAFE mode majority · exposure compression · pyramiding disabled · research → risk analysis.

### Survival Civilization
Conditions: Systemic Risk > 75 · DD > 5% · Liquidity collapse.
Behavior: STOP mode across agents · vault preservation · research only · zero expansion.

**Survival overrides everything.**
