# 🖥 War Room & The Delegation

Two front-ends for the empire.

## Quant War Room
Pixel-art trading command center (recreation of `tradingguru.ai/war-room`). Vite + React. Lives in the active empire workspace at `~/tradingguru-empire/design/tradingguru-ai-corrected/full-site/` and runs on `localhost:5173`.
- Starfield, HUD, live ticker, animated agent office, TradingView remix panel
- 8 in-scene agents: ALPHA · SKEPTIC · EXECUTOR(hub) · REGIME · RISK · HUNTER · CHAMPION(hub) · RECOVERY
- Has a **THE DELEGATION** launch button

## The Delegation
3D agentic-AI playground (`github.com/arturitu/the-delegation`), Three.js WebGPU + Gemini. Lives in `~/tradingguru-empire/the-delegation/` and runs on `localhost:3000/the-delegation/`.
- Hosts the [[Councils_28|28 councils]]
- BYOK Gemini key (entered in-app; validated 200 OK)
