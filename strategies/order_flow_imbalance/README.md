## Signal Logic

### Entry Conditions

**SHORT (Sell):**
- 4+ of last 5 candles are bearish (price drops)
- RSI(14) > 55 (confirms overbought)
- Total confirms: 4 (pressure) + 1 (RSI) = 5

**LONG (Buy):**
- 4+ of last 5 candles are bullish (price rises)
- RSI(14) < 45 (confirms oversold)
- Total confirms: 4 (pressure) + 1 (RSI) = 5

**Minimum Confirms to Open:** 2  
**Trading Modes:** SCALP_SHORT, GRID
